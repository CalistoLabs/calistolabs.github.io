RoleStar

Interactive Application

‹ ›
Tags:
cakephp, amazon-web-services, cloudant, cqrs, domain-driven-design, event-sourcing, ruby, python, ffmpeg, sox, social media, gamification, ui & ux design, art direction

Social video entertainment platform which allowed movie fans to perform scenes from their favorite movies with each other through their webcam. A teleprompter interface delivers lines to the user while they perform against a friend and create their own interpretation of a scene from a classic movie! Users can be nominated for their performances and can even mix their performances with other users to create new content and meet new people.

Built using PHP. Hosted on AWS/RightScale. Storage on S3, Cloudant & MySql. RabbitMQ for service bus.
