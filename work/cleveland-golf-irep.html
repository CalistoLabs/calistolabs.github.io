Cleveland Golf iRep

iPad Application

‹ ›
Tags:
ipad, coca, c, ios, cakephp, ui & ux design

The iRep is an iPad app we designed and developed for the sales team of Cleveland Golf / Srixon. Using a custom built CMS, the sales team is able to create slides, upload images, videos, and html content, and arrange them in any order for a custom presentation slideshow. Each sales rep also has the ability to e-mail a PDF version to a client from the app for any slide during their presentation. With its intuitive interface and convenient content management system, the iRep app has successfully helped the client move away from their traditional printed catalog presentation to a more efficient digital method. In recent months, this project has aided in boosting new accounts by 60%.
